Middle-tech Master

My hobbies gravitate to the wide line between the highs and lows of technology. In this madly and rapidly advancing age of the cutting-edge and next big technological breakthrough, I love finding new places to toy with what are seen as today's basic and fundamental elements, which themselves served as the technological forefront of the recent past.

Pixel Art

With precise control of the smallest bits of space and time, we can build and tear down mountains. This is how I create-- one dot at a time. Seurat spent his Sunday afternoons with points of paint on a canvas. I like moving tiny squares around on the screen.
