I've Been Everywhere, Man...

I help make things... better.

I have a knack for detail and a perfectionistic urge to tinker.

For the past two years, I have been working as a quality assurance engineer and lead at Unqork, a no-code SaaS solution for many Fortune50 companies. Seeing the code side of no-code makes me want to learn more of the code side.
